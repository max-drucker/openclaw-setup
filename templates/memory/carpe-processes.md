# Carpe Data — How We Work

> Internal processes, tools, and workflows.

## Sales Process
1. **Lead Generation** — Inbound (conference, website) + outbound (sales team prospecting)
2. **Discovery** — Understand carrier's pain points, map to Minerva or ClaimsX
3. **Free Pilot** — Retrospective validation (2,000 claims CSV → 4-6 week analysis → executive readout)
4. **Proposal** — Custom pricing based on volume, products, commitment length
5. **Procurement** — Legal/security review, MSA negotiation
6. **Integration** — API, ClaimSearch plugin, or SFTP depending on carrier IT capacity
7. **Go-Live** — Monitoring, calibration, quarterly business reviews

## CRM: Salesforce
- All pipeline tracked in Salesforce
- Key fields: Opportunity Name, Year 1 ARR (primary metric), Stage, Close Date, Owner
- **Use Year 1 ARR for pipeline totals** — NOT Amount (which is TCV/total contract value)
- Stages: Prospecting → Qualification → Needs Analysis → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed Won/Lost

## Communication Tools

## Security & Compliance
- SOC 2 compliant
- Data governance policies for handling PII
- Compliance-safe approach: adjusters never see raw social media
