## Our Core Thesis

Online data — reviews, websites, social media, business listings — reveals hidden risks that traditional insurance data cannot. We mine this data at scale with AI and deliver it as sub-second API responses integrated into carrier workflows.

## Two Product Lines

### 🔍 Minerva — Commercial Underwriting Intelligence
*"Know more about a business than the business knows about itself"*

- **50M+ business datastore** covering 90% of insurable US businesses
- **Carpe Instant:** Sub-second API response — 30+ risk scores, indexes, business characteristics
- **Carpe Thinking:** AI-enhanced matching (1-3 min) — adds 30% more matches, multi-paragraph descriptions, custom appetite prompting
- **Carpe Risk Score:** Composite 1-5 rating — 70% of large losses fall in the bottom 35% of scores
- **NAICS 2.0 Classification:** Up to 5 most likely industry codes with confidence scores
- **200+ Business Characteristics:** Boolean indicators from reviews and descriptions
- **Location Scores:** Address-level proximity and zip-level density risk

**Use Cases:**
- New business triage and flow automation
- Misclassification detection (10% of businesses are misclassified on a $1B book = $3.3M leakage)
- Renewal underwriting and book review
- Pricing model enhancement and segmentation
- Commercial property tenant monitoring
- Lead generation and cross-sell from personal lines

**ROI:** ~$12.2M per $1B premium book (5-10x return)

### 🛡️ ClaimsX — Claims Intelligence & Fraud Detection
*"Monitor 100% of claims, not just the 5-20% you can review manually"*

- **Online Injury Alerts:** Automated, continuous monitoring of injury claimants from FNOL through closure. Evidence-based alerts with curated media capture.
- **Investigative Reports:** On-demand deep dives with AI + human analyst layer. Dynamic tiering (Snapshot → Standard → Pro+) means no-hit-no-charge.
- **Claims Reasoning Engine:** AI layer that qualifies, prioritizes, and recommends next-best-action for every claim. "Continuous assessment" messaging.

**Pricing:**
- Online Injury Alerts: $25-$35 per claimant (volume-tiered)
- Investigative Reports: Dynamic tiering — 50-70% resolve at cheapest Snapshot tier
- 15-20% discount for multi-year commitments

**Use Cases:**
- Fraud detection across all injury claims (not just flagged ones)
- Workers' comp activity monitoring
- Litigation risk reduction
- Settlement acceleration
- Compliance-safe social media research (adjusters never see raw social)

**Lines of Business:** Personal Auto, Commercial Auto, Workers Comp, General Liability, Disability, Malpractice

**ROI:** ~$30.6M annual impact on 95K claims (severity + litigation + settlement speed)
